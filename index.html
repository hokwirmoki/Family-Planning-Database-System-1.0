FAMILY PLANNING DATBASE SYSTEM

Welcome to our website
The Family Planning Database System (FPDS) is a comprehensive web-based application
designed to facilitate the efficient management and organization of family planning information.
The primary goal of FPDS is to generate reports that assist the government in making informed decisions
to curb rapid population growth. The system is built on an Oracle database
